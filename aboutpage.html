About the Event

🏃‍♂️ Carmel Run 2025 – Stride for a Cause! 🏅

The Carmel Run 2025 is more than just a race—it's a movement! Organized by Carmel High School, Basaveshwaranagar, this event is designed to ignite the spirit of fitness, camaraderie, and social responsibility. Whether you’re a seasoned runner or just starting your fitness journey, there's a category for everyone!

🏡 Event Story & Legacy

Carmel Run started as a small initiative by Carmel High School, Basaveshwaranagar, to encourage fitness and community engagement. Over the years, it has grown into a celebrated annual event, bringing together students, families, and fitness enthusiasts.

💡 Past Editions Impact:

  • ✨

    Thousands of participants across different age groups

  • ❄️

    Raised funds for social causes & community well-being

  • 🍃

    Inspired many to adopt a healthier lifestyle

🎯 Why Join Carmel Run?
